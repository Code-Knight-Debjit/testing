Our Partner's Legacy

The Timken Story

Over 125 years of bearing innovation — from horse-drawn wagons to spacecraft. Anupam Bearings is proud to carry this legacy to every corner of India.

Explore Our Products ›
1890s
The Beginning
Henry Timken designs a tapered bearing to help heavy freight wagons make sharp turns — solving a real-world problem that changed industry forever.
1899
Clear Vision
The Timken Roller Bearing and Axle Company is established. The patented tapered roller bearing becomes the bedrock of the company.
1920s
Industrial Focus
Timken supplies bearings to 80% of America's automobiles and begins expansion to new markets across industrial machinery.
1950s
Advanced Processes
Automated, high-volume bearing production drives productivity. Timken continues to accelerate product development and line expansion.
2023
Anupam Partnership
Anupam Bearings begins its Timken partnership — bringing world-class bearing solutions to India's industrial heartland.
